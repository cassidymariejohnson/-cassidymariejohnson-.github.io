World's Best Lemonade

Servings 20

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾C sugar
  • 8 C water
  • 1 ½C lemon juice

Directions

  1. In a sauce pan, combine sugar and 1 C water.
  2. Bring to a boil and stir to dissolve all of the sugar.
  3. Allow to cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate until chilled.
  4. Remove seeds from lemon juice, but leave pulp. In pitcher, stir together chilled syrup, lemon juice and remaining 7 C water.
