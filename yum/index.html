Old Fashioned Lemon Bars

Servings 36

Ingredients

  • 2 C flour
  • 1 t baking soda
  • 1 t ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 t ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 t salt
  • 3/4 C shortening
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 T lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 C packed brown sugar
  • 3 T lemon zest
  • 1 C rasins

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350° Fahrenheit. Grease a 9-inch square baking pan.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, spices and salt and set aside. In a large bowl, cream together shortening and brown sugar. Beat in the eggs one at a time.
  3. Add in the lemon juice and zest. Gradually blend in the dry ingredients. Stir in raisins slowly. Spread batter evenly in the prepared greased baking pan.
  4. Bake 25–30 minutes until lightly colored on top. Let cool in pan. May be drizzled with a simple confectioners' sugar icing. Cut into bars and enjoy!
